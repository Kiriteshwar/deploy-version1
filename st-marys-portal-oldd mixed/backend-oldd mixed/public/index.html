About St. Mary's High School

For over three decades, St. Mary's High School has stood as a pillar of academic excellence, discipline, and value-based education. Rooted in strong traditions and guided by a progressive vision, the school continues to shape young minds in an ever-evolving world.

We are committed to nurturing not only academic achievement but also character, confidence, and social responsibility. Through dedicated faculty, modern learning facilities, and a student-centric approach, we create an environment where every child is encouraged to discover their potential and grow into a responsible, capable individual.
